core beliefs

open communication

Being open to new and different ideas, accepting & giving critique and interacting beyond the game. Also to have healthy discussions the absence of ego on all sides is vital.

team identity

Before starting to counterstrat against your opponents, you should focus on finding your own strengths first. Being comfortable with something and knowing outcomes of different reoccuring scenarios is more valuable than being unpredictable.

intentional gameplay

DOING things, not because they seem or feel right, but instead knowing why and when a play is good. Discussing different decisions and understanding why something is good or not let's players apply that to multiple scenarios in games.

data driven approach

League is a game with countless little decisions of which most are qualitative. However some are quantifiable and you should use available data and tools to figure out those parts of the game.
